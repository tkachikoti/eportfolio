Mid-Module Assignment: System Design - Evaluation

The System Design assignment was a valuable learning experience for me. By using UML to design a new software system to manage self-service checkouts at a supermarket, I was able to apply the concepts and principles of information systems that I had learned in class to a real-world scenario. This helped me to understand how these concepts and principles can be used in practice to design and develop information systems.

One of the positives of completing this assignment was that I was able to improve my skills in using UML to design object-oriented information systems. I was able to use class diagrams, activity diagrams, and state diagrams to represent the different aspects of the system, such as the classes, objects, and the different states of a transaction. This helped me to understand how these different diagrams can be used to represent a system and how they can be used to communicate the design to others.

On the other hand, one of the negatives of completing this assignment was that it required a lot of time and effort to complete. Since the assignment was quite detailed and required a lot of research to understand the requirements of the system, it took me a lot of time to complete the assignment. Indeed, I failed to submit the assignment on time and so I had to send it as a resubmission.

However, the time and effort that I put into completing this assignment helped me to develop, implement, and critically evaluate information system solutions. I was able to use my knowledge and skills to design a system that met the requirements of the assignment and that could facilitate business decisions.

In terms of the impact that completing this assignment had on my personal and professional development, it has helped me to improve my problem-solving and critical-thinking skills. Additionally, working on this project has reinforced my knowledge of software design and development and has given me practical experience in applying these concepts to a real-world scenario. Overall, I believe that this project has been a valuable learning experience and has helped me to develop my skills and knowledge in the field of software engineering.

In conclusion, the assignment was a challenging but valuable learning experience for me. It helped me to improve my skills in using UML to design object-oriented information systems, develop, implement, and critically evaluate information system solutions, and appreciate the concepts and principles of information systems. Overall, the work I produced for this assignment was related to the module learning outcomes and helped me to develop my understanding and skills in information systems.
